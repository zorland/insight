Yelp Rating Algorithm

A rating of 5 with only 1 review should not be trusted!.

Calculates an uncertainty on the rating using a modified version of the wilson score confidence interval.

But which way to scale the score?

Rating is scaled toward the center. Restaurants with few (<40) reviews tend toward the extremes of the rating scale when compared to those with more reviews (>40)
