Dine-amic Foods

Smart Decisions Based on Dynamic Data

Yelp Contains Lots of Information

But it's hard to make efficient use of it

...and so does Google.

Hundreds of results

Dynamic Conditions are Important too

Sunny days are not the same as thunderstorms.

But who has time to wade through this information!

The Solution

Dineamic Foods helps you make the smart choice.

It combines google and yelp ratings data in a smart manner.

Assess the yelp ratings using modified Wilson confidence interval and using the yelp public dataset.

It pulls real time data from the national weather service to bubble up or down weather-appropriate food choices

Resulting in a few choices presented in a simple, easy to use manner.

Technology Details

Using a modified version of the wilson score confidence interval. Calculates an uncertainty on the rating.

Rating is scaled toward the average via the calcuated uncertainty. Restaurants with few (<40) reviews tend toward the extremes of the rating scale when compared to those with more reviews (>40)
